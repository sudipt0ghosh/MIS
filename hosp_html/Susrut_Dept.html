LIST OF DEPARTMENT








Cataract

In normal circumstances the lens of the eye is clear allowing light rays to pass through it easily and focus on the retina to give clear vision.






Retina

This department takes care of the posterior segment of the eye which comprises of this delicate surgery takes place where the gel-like vitreous and light-sensitive membrane (retina) are found. The retina is located approximately two centimeters behind your pupil. It is shaped like a small cup.




Corneal Diseases

The cornea is the transparent window covering the front part of the eye. It focuses light on the retina for clear vision.








Glaucoma

Glaucoma is a group of eye diseases in which the pressure inside the eye (intra ocular pressure/ IOP) rises gradually weakening the optic nerve.






UVEA

The uvea is the middle layer. It’s right between the white part of – called the sclera – and the inner layers eye









LASIK AND REFRACTIVE

The Excimer Laser is one of the greatest inventions in ophthalmology.The Excimer Laser is the device that physically corrects your glass prescription during surgery.









Diabetic Retinopathy

The incidence of diabetes mellitus is on the rise world over, in India more than 60 million people are suffering from it.






Oculoplasty

Oculoplastic surgery is the cosmetic, corrective, and reconstructive surgery of the eye. This department deals with various disorders of orbit, eyelids and Lacrimal system. This department involves the socket in which the eye is placed and also the related structures around the eyes, like eyelid, eyelashes, lacrimal apparatus etc.




Paediatric & Squint

Paediatric refers to the medical (ophthalmological) care of infants, children and adolescents, and the age limit usually ranges from birth up to 18 years
